Historic Assests

As we have witnessed two historic results of MOHD NOOH SIDDIQUI (IRS) and SHAIKH SALMAN PATEL (IRS), the awareness among the masses and the participation of promising candidates have significantly increased. Among the 20 batches, almost 40 students are currently preparing for UPSC CSE 2023 in premium institutions in Delhi and other cities. In the new batch, almost 75 students are preparing for UPSC CSE 2023-24. Due to the constant collective efforts, the results are now visible, and two of our students wrote UPSC CSE 2019 Mains, with one of them (Ammara Siddiqui) appearing for the interview stage. In 2020, three students wrote UPSC CSE 2020 Mains exam, and one student (Mohammed Adil Siddiqui) qualified for the Interview. Similarly, in 2021, five students wrote UPSC CSE 2021 Mains Exam. The foundation's continuous efforts have yielded positive outcomes, inspiring and motivating more students to pursue the UPSC Civil Services Examination, and achieving notable progress over the years

Shaikh Salman (IRS) AIR-339 (UPSC CSE 2017)

Our Activities

  • Guidance Programs

    • Kawish and yash foundation conducts guidance program, motivational and orientation lectures to assist students preparing for UPSC, MPSC and other competitive exams exams. For this purpose, the trust invites many young successful candidates of UPSC, MPSC, and other exams, as well as highly qualified mentors, to guide the students. These programs are organized at various locations within Aurangabad city, providing valuable insights and mentorship to aspiring candidates.

  • Career Guidance Mentorings

    • Kawish and yash Foundation also focuses on career guidance mentoring, helping individuals explore and identify suitable career paths. This initiative aims to provide direction and support to students and young professionals in making informed decisions about their future endeavors.

  • Token for Appreciation

    • Kawish and yash foundation conducts programs to recognize and appreciate talented students who have achieved distinction in their 10th and 12th examinations. This recognition boosts their confidence and serves as a motivation to continue excelling academically.

  • Tree Plantation Program

    • To promote environmental conservation and awareness, the trust organized tree plantation programs frequently , with the collaboration of other civil society and NGO's in this field. This initiative contributes to a greener and healthier environment for the community.

  • COVID-19 Relief Efforts

    • During the lockdown period, Yash Charitable Foundation extended support to impoverished and distressed families by providing relief packages. These activities were carried out in various poor and daily wage earner's localities across different parts of the city, ensuring that those in need received essential supplies and assistance.

    Free Medical Checkup Camps

    Alongside civil societies and other NGOs, the foundation organized free medical checkup camps. These camps primarily focus on backward areas, providing access to healthcare services for individuals who may face barriers in seeking medical attention.

    Academic Activities

    Classroom Activities

    For this purpose the kawish and yash foundation conducted entrance test exams or / screening test to identify deserving candidates for their educational programs. The foundation organizes classroom lectures, guiding students preparing for pre UPSC, MPSC, and other competitive exams. These sessions cover essential exam requirements, study discipline, and also include interactive activities like debate programs, speech competitions, and guest lectures.



    Reading Hall(Study Center)

    The Reading Hall/Study Centre is a collaborative initiative by Kawish and Yasha Foundation in Aurangabad. Established on May 1, 2018, our mission is to provide a dedicated space for students preparing for competitive exams such as UPSC, MPSE, NEET, JEE, NET, and more. Our Reading Hall offers a serene and conducive environment for focused studying, equipped with Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, clean drinking water, and a tranquil atmosphere. Here, students can immerse themselves in their studies, free from distractions, and connect with like-minded peers to achieve academic excellence.



    Donation of Books and Stationery

    Yash Charitable Foundation believes in collaboration and cooperation. In addition to other NGOs, the trust has donated books and stationary items to municipality run schools, primarily focusing on underprivileged areas and localities with daily wage earners



    Scholarships & Library

    The foundation aims to provide equal opportunities to economically weaker sections of society. Yash Charitable Foundation offers scholarships to support financially disadvantaged students, especially those preparing for competitive exams. This initiative empowers students to pursue their education and compete on an equal footing.
    Library:
    From May 1, 2018, the trust established a library section that houses books specifically related to UPSC, MPSC exams, NCERT textbooks, monthly magazines, and newspapers. This resource is available for students to enhance their knowledge and access relevant study materials.



    ❮ ❯

    Affiliation

    In this humongous task, the foundation has collaborated with Zakat Foundation Of India (ZFI), Sir Syed Educational Revolution Mission (SSERM), and Hamdard Study Circle (HSC) in Delhi. The collaboration aims to conduct and facilitate coaching and guidance for students from the Marathwada region, as well as provide support for sending them to institutions such as Jamia Milia Residential Coaching Academy, Jamia Hamdard, Zakat Foundation of India, Sir Syed Educational Revolution Mission, and Hamdard Study Circle in Delhi.
